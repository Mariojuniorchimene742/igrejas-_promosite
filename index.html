Welcome to Churches+

Churches+ is a platform designed to improve communication between church members and their leaders. The application provides information about events, services, schedules, and church activities. It also allows everyone to stay informed through messages, news, and prayer requests.

Application Features

📅 Event Management

Real-time view of church events, services, and activities.

📢 News and Announcements

Important updates and announcements for all members.

🙏 Prayer Requests

A space to share and track prayer requests from the community.
